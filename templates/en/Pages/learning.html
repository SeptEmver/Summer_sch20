Learning

  • Before you start creating your own unique game, we ask you to read a small instruction on the functionality of the constructor.
  • In our constructor, you can create a game field of the desired size (from 6x6 to 16x16), place various elements of the environment on the game field as you want, as well as set the color of these elements and their text image.
  • First, you need to choose the size of the playing field. As mentioned earlier, the width and height of the playing field can not be less than 6 or more than 16 cells.
  • To change a cell, just click on it and select the property of this cell in the window that appears.
  • You can select multiple cells at once that you want to apply the same property to. To deselect all cells, just click the Escape button, and a second click will reset the selection of a specific cell.
  • You can't place more than one player, more than one hatch, and more than one buff on the playing field for HP or damage.
  • If there are more than 3 mobs on the map, you must provide for the presence of a health Potion, otherwise the player may die and pass the level will be impossible.
  • In the character selection window, you can manually enter the desired character to display the selected elements. Next to the character input field, you can also select its color.
  • In order for your settings to apply, you need to click the "Apply" button, and by clicking the "Reset" button, you will reset all settings to their default values.
  • When you finish editing the map, you can select the level name and description, and then save all your changes by clicking the "Save" button.
  • You can use the "Add level" button to access the next levels. Just be careful, when you go from one level to another, all unsaved changes will be lost.

Learning

Before you start creating your own unique game, please read the following instructions for the constructor functionality. You can create a playing field in our constructor the desired size (from 6x6 to 16x16), place various elements of the environment on the playing field so that how you want it, and also set the color of these elements and their text image.

First, you need to select the size of the playing field. As stated earlier, width and height the playing field can not be less than 6 or more than 16 cells. After creating the playing field, you can start editing it. In the character selection window, you can manually enter the desired character as which will display the selected items. Next to the character input field, you can also select it color. In order for your settings to be applied, you need to click the "Apply" button, and then click click "Reset", you will reset all settings to their default values.

To change a cell, just click on it and select the property in the window that appears these are cells. You can select multiple cells at once that you want to apply the same property to. To to cancel the selection of all cells, just click the Escape button, and a second click will reset the selection a specific cell. You can't place more than one player, more than one hatch, or more on the playing field one buff on HP or damage. If there are more than 3 mobs on the map, you must provide the presence of a health Potion, otherwise the player may die and pass the level will be impossible. When you finish editing the map, you can choose the level name and description, and then you must save all your changes by clicking the "Save" button.

After successfully creating your unique first level, you can continue creating your game and you can also create the following levels. Using the "Add level" button, you can open access to the following levels. Just be careful when moving from one level to another, everything unsaved changes will be lost.
